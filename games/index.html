All games

Hard Reset

What do you do when your computer glitches out? You reset it. What do you do when the universe is running out of energy? You reset it. To be more specific, you gather the remaining energy and solid matter to form a device that will reset the universe to a new state at the Big Bang.

The Ruins of Proxima Centauri B

Together with a partner in crime game development, this game is set in the far future, where mankind flew to the stars to explore the endless mysteries of the cosmos. Quite near, on a planet in orbit around Proxima Centauri, they found ancient, alien ruins that demand exploration. It falls to the player to navigate through the strangely still active traps and retrieve an artefact of great power.

Four Horsemen

Less of a game and more a work of interactive fiction. I wrote the first chapter of a story set in an alternative future full of demons and hyper-intensive surveillance from the government. It is possible that this will get further chapters.

EVERYTHING'S ON FIRE

And only you can put it out! All that stands between the foresty landscape and a blazing hellscape is your trusty waterplane. Collect power-ups that help you extinguish the flames before they envelop more of the forest. This games was written with the same group as Minotaur.

n-body

I made this game for the js13k game jam, where the entire game (including assets such as images) had to fit within the space of 13kb. The gameplay is simple, but difficult to master: You can reverse the movement of the probe for a small bit and you can reverse gravity, but you can't steer the probe. This makes gathering the orbiting asteroids tricky (and fun!)

Minotaur

Written as the first team effort into game-making, this game places the player as the minotaur, who has to defend his labyrinth from invading adventurers. Those petty thieves want to steal everything that's not nailed down.

Wall of Text

Sometimes reading a lengthy written work feels like being chased by wall of text. This game provides a small glimpse into the mind of a software developer riddled with fever-dreams when I was sick during participating in a game jam.

IFF

When all you have is a gun that makes friends of enemies, you adapt the best you can. After all, friendly fire can be useful if it's on the enemy's side. Defeat your enemies through the power of double negatives!

Into Hyperspace!

Trucking through space is a hazardous adventure. Dodging the asteroids and plasma balls that litter the hyperspace routes is hard work, but well rewarded. Become one of the many (maybe not quite) glorious freighter pilots that keep the space empire's trade routes busy!
